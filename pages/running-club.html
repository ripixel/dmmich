Bingham Sunday Running Club

A social running and walking group for all abilities. Every Sunday morning in Bingham.

Everyone's Welcome

The Bingham Sunday Running Club was founded by Dean to get more people moving, socialising, and enjoying the outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting your first Couch to 5K, you'll fit right in.

It's not a race — it's a community. We run (and walk!) together, support each other, and finish with a smile.

Sunday 7AM Every week, rain or shine

Free Always

What to Expect

We meet every Sunday morning at 7am in Bingham. Routes vary week to week, typically covering 3-8km depending on the group. There's usually a mix of paces, so nobody gets left behind.

No sign-up needed — just turn up, say hello, and join in. We're a friendly bunch.

For All Abilities

  • Complete beginners and walkers welcome
  • Mixed pace groups — nobody left behind
  • Social, supportive atmosphere
  • No pressure, no judgement
  • Kids and dogs welcome (on leads)
